1. OVERVIEW

WiredWise ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our on-demand IT support services, website (https://wiredwise.com), mobile application, or interact with our customer support team.

By accessing our services, you agree to the terms outlined below. We handle your data with transparency and care, ensuring compliance with applicable privacy laws.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

To deliver seamless IT support, we collect:

  • Personal Details: Name, email, phone number, and billing/shipping addresses.
  • Device & Technical Data: Device type (laptop, smartphone, etc.), operating system, hardware specifications, IP address, error logs, and diagnostic reports.
  • Service Information: Details of technical issues, service history, session recordings (with consent), and support requests.
  • Account & Payment Data: Username, password (encrypted), payment card details (processed securely via third-party gateways), and transaction history.
  • Communications: Records of chats, emails, calls, and feedback submitted to our support team.

We only collect information necessary to provide our services and improve your experience. You have control over what information you share with us.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your data responsibly for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

Provide remote or on-site IT support and troubleshoot technical issues.

Payment Processing

Process payments and manage service subscriptions securely.

Communication

Send service updates, security alerts, or promotional offers (opt-out available).

Service Improvement

Enhance our services through diagnostics, analytics, and training.

We also use your information to secure our systems against fraud, unauthorized access, or cyber threats, and to comply with legal obligations.

4. DATA PROTECTION

Your security is our priority. We implement comprehensive measures to protect your information:

Encryption

Data transmitted and stored using AES-256 encryption

Access Controls

Role-based permissions limit employee access to sensitive data

Network Security

Firewalls, intrusion detection, and regular vulnerability scans

Employee Training

Strict confidentiality agreements and cybersecurity protocols

We ensure all third-party partners (e.g., payment processors) meet GDPR/CCPA compliance standards.

5. DATA RETENTION

We retain your information only as long as necessary for legitimate business purposes:

3 Years

After service termination for legal/accounting needs

7 Years

For payment records to comply with tax regulations

Indefinitely*

Anonymized data for service improvement

*Anonymized data cannot be traced back to individuals and is used solely for analytical purposes.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have significant control over your personal data:

Access & Portability

Access your personal information or request a copy of your data

Correction

Correct inaccuracies or update incomplete information

Deletion

Delete non-essential data (excluding legally mandated records)

Opt-out & Consent

Opt out of marketing or withdraw consent for recordings/diagnostics

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@wiredwise.com. We respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

7. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons.

Important: We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app alerts. Your continued use of our services after such notification constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
